ABOUT US

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship

Welcome to Intex Craft, a proud member of the prestigious Intex Group from India, a brand that has been redefining excellence since 1996. With nearly three decades of industry leadership, the Intex Group has built a legacy of innovation, quality, and trust across multiple sectors. At Intex Craft, we carry forward this tradition by delivering world-class solutions in carpentry and joinery, crafting bespoke wooden structures and furniture, and executing interior fit-outs and turnkey projects with unmatched precision.

Our expertise in retail branding solutions and large-format digital printing helps businesses create a powerful visual impact, while our proficiency in exhibition stands, steel fabrication & metal works, and painting ensures high-quality finishes in every project. We also specialize in indoor signage solutions, enhancing brand visibility and navigation, along with event management services, where we orchestrate seamless, memorable experiences. Driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence, Intex Craft stands as a trusted partner for businesses seeking superior craftsmanship and creative execution.
