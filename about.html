ABOUT US

Designing Spaces. Defining Experiences.

At Intex Craft, we merge creativity with craftsmanship to transform interiors, elevate branding, and build impactful environments. With a passion for precision and a vision for innovation, we turn concepts into reality.

Crafted Spaces, Designed to Inspire

At Intex Craft, we specialize in transforming ideas into dynamic, functional spaces. From custom carpentry and interior fit-outs to signage, printing, and steel structures, we provide end-to-end design and fabrication solutions. We focus on creating tailored environments that reflect your brand and vision, whether it’s for a retail store, office space, or exhibition.

Our team combines creativity, precision, and efficiency to deliver impactful results on time and within budget. We collaborate closely with you to ensure every detail aligns with your goals, ensuring your space is not only visually appealing but also purpose-driven and functional.

AMC Contract (After-Maintenance)

At Intex Craft, we understand that creating a space is just the beginning. Our AMC (After-Maintenance) Contract services ensure that the spaces we craft remain as functional and inspiring as the day they were completed. With a focus on long-term care and upkeep, we are dedicated to preserving the quality and integrity of your environment, giving you peace of mind and lasting value.
